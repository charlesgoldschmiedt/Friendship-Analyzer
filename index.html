how to export your chats
💬 iMessage
  1. Use iMazing (free trial)
  2. Select device → Messages
  3. Export as CSV or JSON
📱 WhatsApp
  1. Open any chat
  2. Tap ··· → More → Export chat
  3. Choose "Without Media"
📸 Instagram
  1. Settings → Your activity
  2. Download your information
  3. Select Messages → JSON format
𝕏 Twitter/X
  1. Settings → Your account
  2. Download an archive
  3. Find direct-messages.js
